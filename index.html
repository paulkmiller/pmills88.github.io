{ Paul Miller } Who What Where Why

My name is Paul Miller, an enthusiastic bearded Full-Stack Web Developer, with an infatuation for Front-End. I'm a goal-oriented team-leader who values dignity, honesty, and curating beautiful web designs into reality by typing on a keyboard. If placed in a team environment, I'm all about collaboration, forming friendships to better facilitate synergy between my coworkers, Nutella, and headphone's blasting music of all genre's.


Want to work together? Have a project in mind? Email me!

Cirrus Weather

A weather application that sends SMS notifications based on changing weather conditions! Made with Ruby, Ruby on Rails, SASS, and Red Bull.

Charity Informer

Donating to charities is awesome. But even with today's technology, it's still quite difficult to come to an informed decision on the fly. Charity Informer was designed with that problem in mind. Made with Ruby, Ruby on Rails, jQuery, SASS, and coffee.
